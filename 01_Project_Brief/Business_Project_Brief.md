## 1. Executive Summary

Nova Retail Group is a fictional multi-branch retail company that sells consumer products across several locations.

The company records sales transactions regularly but lacks a centralized reporting system that allows senior management to monitor revenue, profit, sales growth, regional performance, product performance and customer activity.

Management currently relies on separate spreadsheets and manually prepared reports. This makes reporting slow, increases the risk of errors and makes it difficult to identify important sales trends quickly.

This project will transform the company’s raw sales data into an interactive executive sales performance dashboard using Microsoft Excel, Power Query and Power BI.

The dashboard will provide management with clear, timely and actionable business insights for better decision-making.

---

## 2. Client Background

Nova Retail Group operates multiple retail branches and serves customers across different regions.

The company sells products through several sales representatives and maintains transaction records containing information such as:

- Sales dates
- Products
- Product categories
- Customers
- Sales representatives
- Branches
- Regions
- Quantities sold
- Unit prices
- Sales revenue
- Costs
- Discounts
- Profit

As the business grows, management requires a more professional and automated method of analysing performance.

---

## 3. Business Problem

The company does not currently have a centralized business intelligence dashboard.

Its existing reporting process creates several challenges:

- Sales reports are prepared manually.
- Information is stored in separate spreadsheets.
- Management cannot monitor key performance indicators from one location.
- Comparing branches, regions and sales representatives takes too much time.
- Poor-performing products may not be identified quickly.
- Revenue and profit trends are difficult to track.
- Decision-makers may receive outdated or inconsistent information.
- Manual reporting increases the risk of calculation and data-entry errors.

The business needs a reliable reporting solution that converts transaction data into useful executive insights.

---

## 4. Project Objectives

The main objective is to design an executive sales performance dashboard that gives management a complete view of business performance.

The project will:

1. Consolidate retail sales data into a structured dataset.
2. Clean and transform the data using Power Query.
3. Calculate important sales and profitability measures.
4. Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard.
5. Monitor revenue, cost, profit and sales growth.
6. Compare performance across branches and regions.
7. Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
8. Evaluate sales representative performance.
9. Analyse customer sales activity.
10. reduce the time required to prepare management reports.
11. Support faster and more informed business decisions.

## 7. Key Performance Indicators

The dashboard will include the following KPIs:

| KPI | Business Purpose |
|---|---|
| Total Revenue | Measures total sales generated |
| Total Cost | Measures the cost of products sold |
| Gross Profit | Shows revenue remaining after product costs |
| Profit Margin | Measures profitability as a percentage of revenue |
| Total Orders | Shows the number of sales transactions |
| Units Sold | Measures the total quantity of products sold |
| Average Order Value | Shows average revenue generated per order |
| Sales Growth | Measures change in sales over time |
| Target Achievement | Compares actual sales with the assigned target |
| Total Discount | Measures the value of discounts provided |
| Active Customers | Shows the number of customers purchasing |
| Revenue per Customer | Measures average customer contribution |
| Top Product | Identifies the highest-performing product |
| Top Region | Identifies the strongest geographical market |
| Top Sales Representative | Identifies the best-performing salesperson |

## 9. Technical Approach

The project will follow this process:

### Stage 1 — Business Understanding

Define the business problem, objectives, KPIs, scope and management questions.

### Stage 2 — Data Preparation

Create or obtain the retail dataset and review its structure and quality.

### Stage 3 — Data Cleaning

Correct missing values, duplicate records, inconsistent formats and invalid entries.

### Stage 4 — Data Transformation

Use Power Query to prepare analysis-ready tables and calculated fields.

### Stage 5 — Data Modelling

Build relationships and organise the dataset for efficient reporting.

### Stage 6 — KPI Development

Create measures for revenue, profit, margin, growth, targets and other business indicators.

### Stage 7 — Dashboard Development

Build an interactive executive dashboard in Power BI.

### Stage 8 — Quality Assurance

Test filters, totals, calculations, relationships and dashboard behaviour.

### Stage 9 — Business Insights

Interpret the results and develop actionable recommendations.

### Stage 10 — Portfolio Presentation

Prepare the GitHub case study and Upwork portfolio materials.
